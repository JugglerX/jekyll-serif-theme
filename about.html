About Us

Al Miswak General Trading LLC was established in Dubai, United Arab Emirates in 2014. We grow in the market and build a name as professional solutions provider. We create good client base in different sectors especially in the oil and gas industry.

We also become one of the reliable Local & International Trading companies as exporter and importer dealing with different commodities in large volumes and build great trust relationship with all of our valuable clients. Our client base includes CNPC, CPECC,CNOOC, COSL, SINOPEC, KOC & JO in Kuwait, and others in Middle East and other regions.

Company Vision

To achieve the highest level of recognition as “professional solutions provider” all over the Middle East Oil & Gas market.

Company Mission

To provide quality products & professional services to our clients through years of experiences gained in engineering field and regional market. We are working hard to become your trusted material and services supplier.
